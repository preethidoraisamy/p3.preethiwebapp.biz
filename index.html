Help Section

When you place the symbol in three cells horizontally/ Vertically/ Diagonally then you win. - User can play Tic Tac Toe against the computer, by selecting "Against Computer" checkbox. When you click "New game", the current game will be reset. And the whole game series can be reset by clicking "Reset" button. Number of games played, Tie games, Player 1 and Player 2 status will be displayed
